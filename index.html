How it adapts

Life gets in
the way.

Izzy's coaching moves with you. Most programmes assume your life stays the same every week. Yours does not. Neither does your coaching.

😴
Bad week of sleep
You have been up late all week and you are running on empty.
Izzy sees it in your check-in. Volume comes down. Recovery work goes up. The programme does not punish you for being human — it adjusts so you can keep showing up without burning out.
🤯
Work is full on
You have a deadline, a presentation, three meetings before lunch. The gym is the last thing on your mind.
Tell Izzy in your check-in. Shorter sessions, fewer days, same results over time. The programme contracts around your real week rather than expecting you to train like nothing happened.
✈️
Travelling or no gym access
You are away for a week, in a hotel, or the gym is just not happening.
Izzy switches your programme to bodyweight or hotel-friendly training. Your streak does not break just because your circumstances changed. You keep moving, keep progressing.
🤕
Something hurts
Your knee has been playing up. Your shoulder felt wrong on the last press. You are nervous to push it.
Izzy removes or modifies that movement immediately. You are not training around an injury you told a generic programme about. Izzy knows, and she fixes it before it becomes a problem.
🎉
You are going on holiday
Two weeks in Ibiza, not tracking a thing, living your life.
Izzy factors it in. She builds a bridge plan for around your trip so you do not come back to a programme that expects you to be at week 8 when you feel like week 1. Holidays happen. The coaching accounts for them.
🔥
You are flying
Energy is through the roof, you have hit three PRs this week, you feel unstoppable.
Izzy knows that too. Volume increases, intensity goes up, the programme keeps pace with you. Most plans hold you back when you are ready to push. Yours does not.

FAQ

Every question
answered.

What exactly do I get and how is it delivered?
+
You receive a private web link — your personal coaching portal. Open it in your browser on your phone and it loads full screen like an app. No download, no account, no app store. Inside it is your full personalised training programme, nutrition targets, meal structure, weekly recipes, and a check-in system. Everything was built by Izzy specifically for you based on your intake form. You will receive the link by email within 24 hours of completing the form and payment being confirmed.
Is Izzy actually the one building and updating my programme?
+
Yes. Izzy personally builds every programme and reads every check-in. This is not an AI tool or a template system. When you submit your check-in, Izzy reads it and updates your plan herself. The coaching is personal because it actually is personal.
How is this different from buying a programme PDF or using an app?
+
A PDF is static. An app gives you generic options. What Izzy builds is neither. Your training split, your macro targets, your meal structure, and your recipes are all written specifically for you based on your intake form. Nobody else has your programme. And unlike a PDF it updates every week based on how you are actually progressing — because Izzy reads your check-in and adjusts accordingly. The longer you work with Izzy the more precise and effective your coaching gets.
What does the weekly check-in involve?
+
It takes about 2 minutes. From the Check-in tab in your portal you rate your training, nutrition, sleep, and energy out of 10, and answer a few short questions about how the week felt. Izzy reads every submission and updates your programme based on what you tell her. If energy was low, volume comes down. If you are consistently progressing, the programme gets harder. If life got in the way, Izzy adjusts around it.
I have a really busy schedule. Can this actually work for me?
+
This is built for busy people. Izzy builds your sessions around the days and time you actually have — whether that is 2 days a week for 30 minutes or 5 days with 90 minutes. And if a week comes along where everything falls apart, you tell Izzy in your check-in and she adjusts. The programme is not built around an idealised version of your week. It is built around your real one.
I have dietary restrictions. Can Izzy accommodate them?
+
Yes, completely. The intake form covers dietary restrictions in detail — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, lactose intolerant, halal, no beef, no pork, specific allergies, foods you simply do not like. Your nutrition plan and every recipe Izzy writes is built entirely around what you actually eat. You will not open your portal and find a meal plan full of things you cannot or will not eat.
How does payment work and when am I charged?
+
Payment is handled by Stripe — the same platform used by major subscription services worldwide. At the end of the intake form you are taken to a secure Stripe payment page where you enter your card details. Your first month is charged immediately. Billing then repeats monthly on the same date. Stripe handles all billing automatically and sends you a receipt each month. Your card details are never stored by this website — everything goes through Stripe's encrypted infrastructure.
What is the 3-month minimum commitment?
+
All plans have a 3-month minimum commitment. This exists for two reasons. First, Izzy invests significant time building your programme from scratch before you have your first session — that investment needs to be covered. Second and more importantly, 12 weeks is genuinely the minimum period to see meaningful results from structured coaching. Signing up for one month and leaving before the programme is complete would not serve you. After the 3 months your subscription continues monthly and you can cancel with 30 days written notice at any time.
What is the refund policy?
+
If you change your mind within 48 hours of receiving your portal you can request a full refund. After that period the subscription is non-refundable, because Izzy has already spent her time building your programme specifically for you and that cannot be transferred or reused. If there is a genuine issue with the service Izzy will always hear you out. Full terms are available to read before you sign up — just ask.
What happens after the 3-month programme ends?
+
Your subscription rolls into a new monthly cycle automatically. Izzy builds you a fresh programme for the next phase based on your 12 weeks of progress data. The coaching does not stop — it evolves. If you want to cancel at this point, give 30 days written notice and you are done.
