FAQ

Questions
answered here.

Does it work for my training type?
Yes. Programmes have been built for runners, CrossFitters, cyclists, home trainers, people returning after pregnancy, people over 50, football players, Hyrox athletes, bodybuilders, dancers, and strength lifters — among others. Your training type is the starting point, not a limitation.
What exactly do I get?
A private coaching portal delivered as a link to your phone. It contains your personalised training plan, nutrition targets, meal structure, six recipes built around your dietary preferences, and a weekly check-in tab. Everything inside is built from your intake form. No shared templates, no generic content.
How does the payment process work?
Fill in the intake form. Once reviewed you will receive a payment link directly. Portal only is £47 one-time. Portal plus weekly coaching is £47 + £67/month. Your portal goes into build the moment payment is confirmed.
How long until I receive my portal?
Within 24 hours of payment being confirmed. You receive a private link by email.
How does the weekly check-in work?
Every week you open your portal, go to the Check-in tab, and rate your training, nutrition, sleep, energy, and mood. Your programme updates based on what you report. If energy is low, volume adjusts. If you are hitting PRs, load increases. It responds to what is actually happening.
Do I need to download anything?
No. Your portal is a private web link. Open it in your phone browser. It loads full screen and works like an app. No account, no download, no app store required.
Can I cancel?
The portal is a one-time payment — it is yours forever, no cancellation needed. The weekly coaching subscription is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. No minimums, no contracts.
