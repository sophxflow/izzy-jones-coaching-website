How it adapts

Life gets in
the way.

Your system moves with you. Most plans assume every week is the same. Yours does not.

😴
Bad week of sleep
You have been up late all week and you are running on empty.
You log it in your check-in. Volume comes down. Recovery focus goes up. The system does not punish you for being human.
🤯
Work is full on
Deadline week. Three meetings before lunch. The gym feels impossible.
You tell us. Sessions get shorter, frequency drops for the week. The system contracts around your real week rather than expecting you to train like nothing happened.
✈️
Travelling or no gym
Away for a week. Hotel room. No equipment.
Your system switches to bodyweight or hotel-friendly sessions. Your streak does not break just because your circumstances changed.
🤕
Something hurts
Your knee is playing up. Your shoulder felt wrong on the last press.
Log it and that movement gets removed or modified immediately. You are not training around an injury you mentioned once and that a generic plan ignored entirely.
🎉
Holiday coming up
Two weeks away. Not tracking anything. Living your life.
A bridge plan gets built around your trip so you do not come back to a system that expects you to be at week 8 when you feel like week 1. Holidays happen. The system accounts for them.
🔥
You are flying
Three PRs this week. Energy through the roof. You want more.
Volume increases. Intensity goes up. The system keeps pace with you. Most plans hold you back when you are ready to push. Yours does not.

FAQ

Every question
answered.

What exactly do I get and how is it delivered?
+
You receive a private web link — your personal coaching portal. Open it in your browser on your phone and it loads full screen like an app. No download, no account, no app store. Inside is your full personalised training system, nutrition targets, meal structure, weekly recipes, and a check-in system. Everything was built specifically for you based on your intake form. The link arrives by email within 24 hours of completing the form and payment being confirmed.
How is this different from a generic system or a fitness app?
+
A generic system is the same for everyone. A fitness app gives you options from a library. This is built from scratch around what you told us about your life — your schedule, your body, your food, your goals. Nobody else has your system. And unlike something static, it updates every week based on how you are actually progressing. The longer you use it, the more precise it becomes.
How does the weekly check-in work and what happens after I submit?
+
It takes about 2 minutes. From the Check-in tab in your portal you rate your training, nutrition, sleep, and energy out of 10, and answer a few short questions about how the week felt. Your system then updates based on what you reported. If energy was low, volume comes down. If you are consistently hitting PRs, the system gets harder. If life got in the way, it adjusts around it. The update happens automatically and your portal reflects it.
I have a really unpredictable schedule. Will this actually work for me?
+
This is specifically designed for people with unpredictable lives. Your system is built around the days and time you actually have — whether that is 2 days with 30 minutes or 5 days with 90 minutes. And when a week comes along where everything falls apart, you log it in your check-in and the system adjusts for that week. It is not built around an idealised version of your life. It is built around the real one.
I have dietary restrictions. Can you accommodate them?
+
Yes, completely. The intake form covers dietary restrictions in full — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, lactose intolerant, halal, no beef, no pork, specific allergies, foods you simply do not like. Your nutrition plan and every recipe that arrives each week is built entirely around what you actually eat. You will never open your portal and find something you cannot or will not eat.
How does payment work and when am I charged?
+
After submitting the form you will receive a payment link by email within a few minutes. Click it, complete payment on the secure Stripe page, and your portal gets built. Stripe bills you automatically on the same date each month and sends a receipt every time. Your card details are never stored by this website — everything runs through Stripe's encrypted infrastructure.
Why is there a 3-month minimum commitment?
+
Two reasons. First, your system is built from scratch specifically for you — that takes real time and cannot be reused if you leave after a week. Second, and more importantly, 12 weeks is genuinely the minimum period to see meaningful results from structured coaching. The system is designed as a complete 12-week journey. Leaving before that is complete would shortchange your own results. After the 3 months it rolls monthly and you can cancel with 30 days notice.
What is the refund policy?
+
If you change your mind within 12 hours of receiving your portal you can request a full refund. After that period the subscription is non-refundable, because your system has already been built specifically for you. If there is a genuine issue with the service, get in touch and it will be heard. Full terms are available to read before you sign up.
What happens after the 12 weeks?
+
Your subscription rolls into a new monthly cycle. A fresh system gets built for the next phase using everything learned from your first 12 weeks — your progress data, your check-ins, what worked and what did not. The coaching does not stop after 12 weeks, it evolves. If you want to cancel at this point, give 30 days written notice and you are done.
